# SaaS Voiceover & Narration Script
**Location: demo/narration.md**

---

### Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)
> *"Welcome to CompensaIQ, a high-performance, enterprise-grade compensation and salary management workspace designed to replace insecure, slow, and fragile HR spreadsheets. Let's sign in to see the administrative console."*

---

### Scene 2: Executive Dashboard (0:30 - 1:30)
> *"Here on the executive dashboard, HR specialists are greeted with real-time aggregates: total payroll costs, global headcount distribution, and alert cards highlighting underpaid or premium salary bands. Every chart and metric is backed by a Neon serverless database, responding under 15 milliseconds."*

---

### Scene 3: Search & Directory (1:30 - 3:00)
> *"Navigating to the employee directory, we can filter our entire workforce using multi-dimension configurations. CompensaIQ features three custom search modes: Starts With, Exact Match, and Contains, mapped directly to PostgreSQL indexing and Elasticsearch queries. Our lists use cursor-based pagination and virtual rendering to maintain 60 FPS scrolling across datasets."*

---

### Scene 4: Salary Revision Auditing (3:00 - 4:00)
> *"Selecting an employee profile opens a sliding workspace. Instead of overwriting past records, CompensaIQ appends modifications to a historical timeline. Every transaction creates an immutable audit trail, tracking user identity and before/after data snapshots."*

---

### Scene 5: Document Management (4:00 - 5:00)
> *"Under the documents tab, HR administrators can upload documents, attach category tags, and toggle confidentiality flags. Document PDFs preview inline inside the browser, completely avoiding local file downloads."*

---

### Scene 6: Bulk Uploads & Validation (5:00 - 6:00)
> *"Uploading bulk lists is safeguarded by our client-side CSV validator. When dropping a CSV file, Zod schemas inspect rows. Errors are flagged inline with hover tooltips, preventing database corruption."*

---

### Scene 7: Grounded AI Assistant (6:00 - 7:00)
> *"Finally, our AI query assistant uses Llama 3 classification to let administrators query payroll structures in plain English. Inputs are parsed into safe search criteria, blocking SQL injection attacks while retrieving aggregates."*

---

### Scene 8: Wrap-up (7:00 - 7:30)
> *"CompensaIQ delivers a scalable, highly secure platform ready for modern workforce management. Thank you for watching."*
